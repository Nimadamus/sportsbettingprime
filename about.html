What We Do

Sports Betting Prime is a data-driven platform that aggregates expert picks from some of the most respected handicappers in the sports betting industry. We monitor daily pick contests and track which games receive the most agreement among proven winners.

Our mission is simple: identify when consensus forms among sharp bettors and deliver that information to you in a clear, actionable format. We don't sell picks. We don't have a "lock of the year." We just show you where the smart money is pointing.

The Power of Consensus

When 70+ professional handicappers all land on the same side of a game, that's not coincidence - it's signal. Our consensus data helps you see when the market's sharpest minds agree, giving you an edge that casual bettors simply don't have access to.

How It Works

Every day, we aggregate data from major handicapping contests where professional bettors compete for prizes. These aren't random picks - they're selections made by people with skin in the game and proven track records.

Our system tracks:

  • Pick Volume - How many experts are on each side of a game
  • Consensus Strength - The percentage agreement on any given play
  • Line Movement - How odds change in response to sharp action
  • Historical Performance - How high-consensus plays have performed over time
