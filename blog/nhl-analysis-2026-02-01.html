The Lightning are a staggering 16-2 SU in their last 18 games and 7-0 SU in their last 7 at home. Jon Cooper has his team playing elite hockey.

Boston (32-20-3) has been solid but inconsistent. The Bruins are 10-2 SU in their last 12 games, showing they can compete with anyone when focused. However, they're 2-5 SU in their last 7 games against Tampa Bay, a troubling trend in this rivalry.

The under is 7-1 in Tampa Bay's last 8 games. The under is 4-1 in the last 5 Bruins-Lightning meetings at home for Tampa.

Outdoor games are unpredictable, but some trends hold. Scoring tends to be lower due to sun glare, wind, and unfamiliar ice conditions. The under 6.5 is the smart play here. Tampa Bay is the better team and should win 4-3 or 3-2. Lightning moneyline with confidence.
