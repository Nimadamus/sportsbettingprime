PITCHING FORENSICS

This is the model's highest-rated edge of the day, centered around a clear pitching mismatch. All data points align for a dominant Yankees performance.

Baltimore Orioles @ Tampa Bay Rays

PITCHING FORENSICS

Corbin Burnes (BAL) has been elite, maintaining a **2.95 xFIP**. However, his Hard Hit Rate on the road (**41%**) is a minor concern. The Rays counter with Taj Bradley, who flashes elite strikeout potential (**29.5% K-rate**) but struggles with command (**9.8% BB-rate**), leading to a volatile **4.35 SIERA** and a high 1.8 HR/9 rate.

OFFENSIVE & SITUATIONAL MATRIX

The Orioles rank **1st in MLB in ISO (.215)** against RHP, a nightmare matchup for a homer-prone pitcher like Bradley. The Rays' offense is league-average (**101 wRC+**), but their bullpen has been overworked, posting a **4.50 ERA** in the last 14 days with their two high-leverage arms having pitched on back-to-back days. Our model projects a high probability of early runs against Bradley and late runs against a tired Rays bullpen.
