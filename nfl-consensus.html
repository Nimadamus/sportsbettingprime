What is NFL Consensus Betting?

NFL consensus picks show you where the professional handicappers are putting their money. When 20+ experts agree on an NFL spread, moneyline, or total, it signals strong conviction from proven winners.

Our NFL consensus data includes:

  • Spreads (ATS): Point spread picks against the spread
  • Totals: Over/under picks on game totals
  • Moneylines: Straight-up winner picks

We track every NFL game from the regular season through the Super Bowl, giving you consensus data for Thursday Night Football, Sunday's full slate, and Monday Night Football.

How to Use NFL Consensus Picks

The best way to use NFL consensus is as confirmation of your own analysis. If you like the Chiefs -3.5 and see that 25 experts also took the Chiefs, it validates your thinking.

Key tips for using NFL consensus:

  • High consensus (15+) picks tend to have value, but lines may adjust
  • Check consensus early - lines move as sharp money comes in
  • Compare consensus across different sportsbooks
  • Don't blindly follow - use it as one data point
